## Introduction to Large Language Models

I learned that Large Language Models (LLMs) are core models of generative AI. Moreover, they are more than language generators as they are also seen as reasoning engines that can become the brains of intelligent applications.

LLMs are a subfield of Large Foundation Models (LFMs) which are a type of generative AI model that are highly versatile, as they are adaptable for various specific tasks. 

Training LFMs is not just limited to textual data, but also includes other formats such as audio, video and imgaes.
